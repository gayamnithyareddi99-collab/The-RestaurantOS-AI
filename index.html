Root Cause Analysis

Snapshot Data Problem

Most UK dough businesses rely on wall thermometers and clocks — failing to account for the non-linear biological activity of yeast. "Night-time compressor lag" and seasonal humidity create invisible black boxes.

FIFO vs FRFO Failure

Rigid "first-in, first-out" protocols lead to disposal of perfectly viable dough "expired" by the clock, while over-proofed, acidic dough reaches the oven — causing poor crumb structure and customer dissatisfaction.

Institutional Craft Loss

Expert "dough-handling logic" walks out the door with every senior baker departure. The UK hospitality sector's "competency cliff" means quality knowledge is never preserved as institutional equity.
